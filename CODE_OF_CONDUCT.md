# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the A2X community a harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

A2X is a project at the frontier of artificial cognition. The work we do here will shape how machines think for decades to come. **The standard of our conduct must match the ambition of our work.** Professional, direct, constructive — always.

---

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be professional.** Communicate with precision and clarity. Assume good faith.
- **Be constructive.** Critique ideas, not people. Every comment should move the discussion forward.
- **Be inclusive.** Different perspectives make the project stronger. Welcome newcomers.
- **Be accountable.** Own your mistakes. Correct them. Learn from them.
- **Be rigorous.** Precision matters in a programming language. Hold the standard.

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Sexual attention or advances of any kind in project spaces
- Disruptive behavior that derails productive discussion
- Submitting malicious code or intentionally compromising the project
- Using AI agents to spam, harass, or circumvent community standards

---

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

### Enforcement Process

1. **Report** — Contact the maintainers via `conduct@coldstart.dev` (or open a private security advisory).
2. **Review** — Reports are reviewed within 48 hours. All reports are confidential.
3. **Action** — Based on severity, actions may include:
   - **Correction:** Private warning, clarification of standards
   - **Warning:** Public or private warning with consequences for continued behavior
   - **Temporary Ban:** Removal from project spaces for a specified period
   - **Permanent Ban:** Indefinite removal from all project spaces
4. **Appeal** — Decisions may be appealed within 30 days by contacting the maintainers.

### Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all A2X community spaces (GitHub, discussions, chat, events) and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.
