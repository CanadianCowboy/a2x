## How to sign

### Individual contributors

Comment on your Pull Request when prompted by the CLA bot:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

The bot records your GitHub username in `signatures/version1/cla.json`. You only sign once — subsequent PRs are auto-approved for CLA status.

### Corporate contributors (employees contributing on behalf of a company)

If your employer holds intellectual-property rights to your work, please have an authorized signatory contact **mail@josh-lynes.com** with a Corporate CLA before contributing. This ensures a clean chain of authorization for your contributions.

Sample Corporate CLA text is available on request.
